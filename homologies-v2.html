HOMOLOGIES

"In the near future, parents who have paid to genetically enhance their children wonder if they have made a terrible mistake."

Directed by Bryerly Long and Produced by Tatiana Blackington James, Homologies is an award-winning sci-fi short film.

The film's accolades include the Audience Award for Best Short Drama at the Sherman Oaks Film Festival, and Best Short Script at the Los Angeles International Screenplay Awards.

The score utilises electronic ambience to create mood and texture, alongside traditional orchestration to highlight key emotive moments as the drama and mystery unfold.
