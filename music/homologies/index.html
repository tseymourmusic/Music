HOMOLOGIES

"In the near future, parents who have paid to genetically enhance their children wonder if they have made a terrible mistake."

Homologies is an award-winning sci-fi short film Directed by Bryerly Long and Produced & Written by Tatiana Blackington James.

The score establishes a strange yet inviting sci-fi atmosphere via melodic swells of cinematic ambient synths. As the mystery unfolds, traditional orchestral textures and haunting melodies rise to the forefront, building tension and emotion toward a shocking reveal.
