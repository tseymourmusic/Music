SAVIOUR

"For string orchestra and piano. A 17-track spiritual journey from birth, sacrifice to immortality"

SESSION SHOTS

Saviour for string orchestra and piano is the debut classical album from New Forest-based composer Tim Seymour.

Recorded in August 2024 at St Saviour's Church in Brockenhurst and released on 18th April 2025, the album was conducted by Phil Daish-Handy and performed by the Nova Foresta Philharmonic featuring solos from cellist Lionel Handy, violinist Catherine Lawlor and pianist Dr Robert Markham.

Comprising 17 tracks spanning 56 minutes, the album received its live premier on 1st Nov 2025 at St Thomas Church, Lymington, performed by the original recording ensemble.
