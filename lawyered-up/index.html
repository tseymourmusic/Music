LAWYERED UP

"In the near future, AI has infiltrated all aspects of human life. When a betrayed woman finds solace in the arms of her seemingly perfect divorce lawyer, she must confront the unsettling truth that perfection always comes at a price."

Written and Directed by Tatiana Blackington James, Lawyered Up is an award-winning sci-fi short film and a 2026 Cannes Emerging Film Maker Showcase finalist.

The original score creates a broad and unique sonic landscape via a fusion of electronic dance, futuristic synths, and classical acoustic instruments, bringing the not-too-distant futuristic world and its diverse cast of characters to life.
