TalES fOr All TiME

A Cinematic Audio-Visual Odyssey featuring the London Symphony Orchestra

Tales for All Time is an ambitious cinematic reimagining of ancient myths, recorded with the London Symphony Orchestra at LSO St Luke’s.

By grounding universal legends in the moss-drenched textures of the New Forest, the project symphonises rich orchestral heritage with contemporary production and 2.5D animated visual art.
