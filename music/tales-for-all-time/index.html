The Odyssey

A cinematic reimagining of 15 ancient myths recorded with the London Symphony Orchestra at St Luke's.

Grounding universal legends in the moss-drenched textures of the New Forest, Tales For All Time is a collaboration between composer Tim Seymour and painter Courtney Leggett.

Each musical movement is accompanied by an evolving 2.5D animated tableau, breathing life into physical art.
