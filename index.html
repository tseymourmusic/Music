CHAPTER I

Tim Seymour | Cinematic Composer Biography

Tim Seymour is an award-winning composer and producer based in the New Forest, England. His compositions symphonise rich orchestral heritage with contemporary cinematic production, creating unique and immersive sonic landscapes rooted in emotive, modern British Pastoral melodies.

A graduate of the European Academy of Fine Arts, Tim’s craft has been shaped by elite mentorship from Andy Hill and Dr. Norman Ludwin. This foundation is bolstered by his professional background in project management (ACMA), ensuring artistic depth with technical precision.

A former LSO SoundHub Associate Composer, Tim is currently architecting his most ambitious work to date: TalES fOr All TiME, scheduled for recording with the London Symphony Orchestra in 2026.
