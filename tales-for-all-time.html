TALES FOR ALL TIME

A CINEMATIC AUDIO-VISUAL ODYSSEY FEATURING THE LONDON SYMPHONY ORCHESTRA 15 GREEK MYTHS REIMAGINED THROUGH THE ENCHANTED LANDSCAPES OF THE NEW FOREST COMPOSED & PRODUCED BY TIM SEYMOUR ILLUSTRATED BY COURTNEY LEGGETT
COMING 2026 / 2027
MOVEMENT XIV: DESTINY

TALES FOR ALL TIME IS A CINEMATIC REIMAGINING OF 15 ANCIENT MYTHS RECORDED WITH THE LONDON SYMPHONY ORCHESTRA AT ST LUKE'S.

GROUNDING UNIVERSAL LEGENDS IN THE MOSS-DRENCHED TEXTURES OF THE NEW FOREST, THE PROJECT IS A COLLABORATION BETWEEN COMPOSER TIM SEYMOUR AND PAINTER COURTNEY LEGGETT.

EACH MUSICAL MOVEMENT IS ACCOMPANIED BY AN EVOLVING 2.5D ANIMATED TABLEAU, BREATHING LIFE INTO PHYSICAL ART TO CREATE AN EMOTIVE & IMMERSIVE AUDIO-VISUAL CINEMATIC EXPERIENCE.
